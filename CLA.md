## How to sign

We use [CLA Assistant](https://cla-assistant.io/) to collect signatures inline with pull requests. When you open your first PR, the CLA Assistant bot will comment with a link — click it, sign in with GitHub, and your signature is recorded. One signature covers all your future PRs.

## What if I don't want to sign?

That's fine. We can still review and discuss your PR. If we want to merge it, we'll explicitly note that the contribution stays AGPL-only — it won't be relicensable to commercial customers. In practice that means we may decline contributions to load-bearing parts of the engine, but documentation, bug fixes, and isolated check additions are usually accepted on those terms.
